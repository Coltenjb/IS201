Lean Production

Lean production is a systematic manufacturing method used for eliminating waste within the manufacturing system.

It takes into account the waste generated from uneven workloads and overburden and then reduces them in order to increase value and reduce costs.

The word ”lean” in the term simply means no excess, so lean production can be translated simply into minimal waste manufacturing.
